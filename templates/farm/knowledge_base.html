Knowledge Base

Best Practices
Soil Management
  • Test soil pH regularly (optimal range: 6.0-7.0)
  • Rotate crops to maintain soil fertility
  • Use organic compost to improve soil structure
Water Management
  • Water early morning or late evening
  • Monitor soil moisture levels regularly
  • Use drip irrigation for water efficiency
Farming Tips
Pest Control
  • Use natural predators when possible
  • Monitor crops regularly for early detection
  • Practice crop rotation to break pest cycles
Fertilization
  • Apply fertilizers based on soil test results
  • Use slow-release fertilizers for better absorption
  • Avoid over-fertilization to prevent nutrient runoff
Government Subsidies
Available Programs
  • Farm Input Subsidy Program
  • Agricultural Equipment Financing
  • Crop Insurance Schemes
How to Apply

Contact your local agricultural extension office for information on available subsidies and application procedures.
